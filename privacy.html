Privacy Policy

1. Human Rights & Data Protection

We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. Your data belongs to you. We operate under the principle of data minimization: we only collect what is absolutely necessary to provide our services to you, and we treat your information with the utmost respect and security, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and international human rights standards.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it to us, for example, when you use our booking form or contact us via email. This data may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Information provided in your message or booking request

3. Purpose of Use

Your data is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Processing your booking requests
  • Communicating with you regarding your sessions
  • Fulfilling our contractual obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any incorrect data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: Circle.D.Flow@gmail.com

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Google Calendar for bookings) to facilitate our operations. These providers have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We ensure that any third-party partners respect your data rights.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us remember your preferences (such as theme settings or booking progress) and understand how you interact with our site.

We may also use third-party cookies from services like Stripe (for secure payments) and Google (for calendar integration). You can control your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner.
